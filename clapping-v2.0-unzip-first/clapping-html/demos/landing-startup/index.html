Features

Event-based Shoutout

Employees can give a shoutout to their colleagues even for a small event.

  • A small event at work that impressed colleagues.
  • Giving shoutouts regularly can increase employee productivity.
  • Shoutouts can highlight achievements and moments when someone learns a new skill, solves a problem creatively, or adapts to challenges.

Shoutout Motivation

People often don't give shoutouts for positive events due to a lack of motivation.

  • Writing a shoutout in three or more lines can be difficult due to thinking of what to say, language skills, or time constraints.
  • At clapping.me, we use stickers to make this task easy and quick.
  • It takes less than a minute to give a shoutout on clapping.me.
  • This results in more shoutouts for employees' soft skills.

Identity
Masking

Revealing the identities of colleagues who give shoutouts can pressure those who don't.

  • The name of the colleague who gave the shoutout will be masked.
  • It creates a healthy environment without bias against those who don't give shoutouts.
  • Employers will get access to the unmasked version.


Personal Branding

As personal branding gains importance, it's becoming essential.

  • Traditional careers are declining; freelance and contract work are increasing, necessitating self-marketing.
  • Online competition is intense; a strong personal brand helps you stand out and establish expertise.
  • Even introverts can gain visibility with a strong personal brand.

Contextual Analysis

Jealous colleagues might undermine positive shoutouts.

  • They may falsely red flag genuine shoutouts.
  • We'll use machine learning to analyze shoutouts and eed flags, detecting malicious activity.
  • Periodic audits will help identify and correct misuse.


Recruitment Analytics

Employers need candidates with strong soft skills.

  • The dashboard highlights employees' top soft skills.
  • Analysis of shoutouts and red flags gives a holistic employee view.
  • Clapping.me identifies top candidates based on consistent praise and few red flags.
How It Works
1. Employee Requests a Shoutout

Upon logging into the website, employees are directed to the 'Request a Shoutout' page, where they can send requests to their colleagues via WhatsApp.

2. Colleague Gives a Shoutout Quickly

Colleagues can easily give a shoutout by mentioning the context and selecting an appropriate sticker, all within a minute.

3. Showcased on Personal Landing Page

All received shoutouts are displayed on the employee's personal landing page. This page can be shared in resumes via a QR code or included in email footers.
